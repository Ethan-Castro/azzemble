Taking a GA P Year

Aug 13 2022 3 min read

A brief encapsulation of what’s to come.

Hey everyone,

I’m back.

This will be a short piece because I know the one I want to write will take much longer and probably won’t be published.

Updates on me:

  • Got Signed as a Model with Fusion Models
  • I’ve been working full-time as a group teacher at a Summer School Site
  • I’ve been focusing on self-learning Data Science
  • About to retake my SAT and start up my college [re]application process

As you know, I shot my shot at some pretty prestigious colleges… I missed just about every shot.

Not to worry. I have a plan.

Instead of settling for the options that presented themself, I decided to take a gap year (with some consulting and my parent’s permission, of course).

I’ve decided to pivot career paths and focus on Data Science.

I believe Data Science is the tangible STEM field that will help me merge all of my passions.

I plan on focusing my youth on getting world-class at analyzing problems from as many perspectives as possible and prioritizing my later years on actually solving these problems. [This isn’t to say I won’t start or join a million startups, write ten more books, and record a million more podcast episodes while I’m still young.]

I want to build an online persona around this Gap Year. With that said, I will be SUPER active on social media platforms in the coming months. They are linked below.

I know this is all risky.

Cheers & Wish me Luck,
Ethan Castro

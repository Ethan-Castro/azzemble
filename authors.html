Ethan Castro

Gap Year Founder & Author

Hey everyone,

My name is Ethan and I am the creator of Azzemble. As you may know, I have grown to love writing and it is one of my predominant leisurely activities/ Many of my friends also desired to express their thoughts, impressions, and insights through writing, but didn't know where to go or how to start. I decided to create Azzemble.
My intention is for this to act as a bridge between what Gen Z is and what they could be. There are a lot of solutions, ideas, and innovations that are being left on the table because we are not actively searching for a way to pull it out of Gen Z. I am going to try my best to bring out our best.


I went to Brooklyn Technical High School where I played football, wrestled, threw for track and field, and some more. Over the course of my four years there I: broke four New York State powerlifting records, took 2nd in NYC fresh/soph Wrestling Championship, wrote & published a book, hosted a podcast, founded & incoporated a youth organization that was contracted to work with Students in Temporary Housing met my closest friends, and learned many lessons (saved the best for last).


I hope over the course of the next few months I am able to make a small impact on at least one person.

Azzemble

Working on creating my own blog site, pivoting my writing from other platforms to this one, then scaling. End goal: Assemble the greatest minds of Gen Z to help LiBerate (Lift up and Build upon each other).

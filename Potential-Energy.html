Potential Energy

Aug 31 2022 3 min read

I am at a very weird point in my life [or Gap Year at least.

Many things are seemingly going well, and I have many opportunities afforded to me, but nothing is happening.

As you may know, I recently got signed to an agency, but I have only been to one casting.

I have a bodybuilding show coming up, but at this point, all I am doing is working out like a maniac and watching my macros.

I am back at jiu-jitsu; there are tournaments in sight, but I am still getting back in the groove.

Studying Python & Excel, but I am still at the beginner stage, where I am sort of useless for the next few weeks before I get a decent grasp of the basics.

I mean, many people my age are in this position, but they have college to distract them from this quarter-life crisis.

College gives them a sense of identity, they are a full-time student living ON campus. Whenever you question yourself, you can just say, “Oh, I’m in college”, no more elaboration is needed other than your year and major.

Anytime I tell anyone what I am doing, I have to either hold the L or go on a long tangent about how I pivoted I am brand new to everything, so the ball is not really rolling in any facet of my life.

Here’s the good thing, although this is the case, it is not the case. This is all in my head.

When I remove myself from the situation and look at it as an outsider, I realize I clearly have a great deal of potential energy, although not apparent to me [when I look in the mirror] because it isn’t kinetic.

All in all, nothing is going on, but everything is about to happen.

Ethan Castro

Some random dude who is somehow having an effective gap year.

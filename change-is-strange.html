Change is Strange

Sep 5 2022 3 min read

Maybe the change you’ve endured has made you exponentially happier, and you find it strange how you went your whole life leading a dull day-to-day lifestyle.

Maybe the change you’ve endured has made your life exponentially more dull & depressing, and you find it strange how you never realized how great life was before now.

Regardless of what it is, you have to reflect on why during X period, things were good or why Y period is currently going great.

You are given sensations, feelings, & emotions while here on earth.

Why just let them happen?

We are at a point in time (maybe not in your current age or life experience)where we can pattern recognize what environments, systems, schedules, and personalities work for us and which don’t.

So why just let things happen to you, and you fall collateral victim or victor to actions that have taken place?

Stoicism is great… for most… during a period of their life.

But we tend to forget that we have some control over how we feel, what happens to us, and what happens because of us.

I am currently at a point of change, but I am not putting it in a category of ‘good’ or ‘bad’, just strange and then doing what I have to do.

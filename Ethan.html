Hey everyone,

My name is Ethan and I am the creator of Azzemble. As you may know, I have grown to love writing and it is one of my predominant leisurely activities/ Many of my friends also desired to express their thoughts, impressions, and insights through writing, but didn't know where to go or how to start. I decided to create Azzemble.

My intention is for this to act as a bridge between what Gen Z is and what they could be.

There are a lot of solutions, ideas, and innovations that are being left on the table because we are not actively searching for a way to pull it out of Gen Z.

I am going to try my best to bring out our best. I went to Brooklyn Technical High School where I played football, wrestled, threw for track and field, and some more.

Over the course of my four years there I:

  • Broke four New York State Powerlifting Records
  • Took 2nd in NYC fresh/soph Wrestling Championship
  • Wrote & Published a Book
  • Hosted a Podcast
  • Founded & Incoporated a Youth Organization
  • Met my Closest Friends
  • Learned Many Lessons (saved the best for last)



    • I hope over the course of the next few months I am able to make a small impact on at least one person.
